Admissions at Benbess Star Academy

We are delighted that you are consiering Benbess Star Academy for your child's education. Our admission process is simple, transparent and designed to welcome students who are ready to learn, grow and succeed.
WE aim to make the enrollment process smooth for parents while ensuring every child is placed in the right learning environment.

📌Admission Requirement

To enroll your child, please prepare the following documents:

  • Birth Certificate (Copy)
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Previous school report
  • Transfer letter(for transfer students)
  • Completed admission form

📖How to apply

Follow these simple steps to apply

  • 1. Visit the school or contact us online
  • 2. Fill out the admission form
  • 3. Submit required document
  • 4. Attend a short interview or assessment
  • 5. Recieve admission confirmation

👉Our team is always available to guide you through each step

📆Admission Period

Admission is open every working day.

💰School fees

Our fees structure is affordable and designed to provide quality education at a reasonable cost.
👉For detailed fee information, please contact the school office.

👶 Classes Available

We offer admission into the following levels:

  • Nursery
  • Kindergarten
  • Primary school
  • Junior High School

Each level is structured to meet the developmental needs of the child.

🏫School Facilites

Students admitted to Benbess Star Academy enjoy access to:

  • Well-equiped classroom
  • Safe and secure environment
  • Learning materials and resources
  • Playground and activity areas
